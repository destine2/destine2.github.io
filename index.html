At CEOT Sumbak Farms, we pride ourselves on over seven years of experience in rearing layers, broilers, pigs, and now specializing in the best genetic breeds of goats and Dorper sheep. Our farm, located in the tranquil landscapes of Rufunsa, Lusaka district, Zambia, exemplifies excellence in sustainable farming practices and animal welfare. We are dedicated to providing top-quality goat breeds, Dorper sheep, and egg products to both local and international markets, ensuring our customers receive superior products at affordable prices. Our commitment to quality and sustainability is reflected in every aspect of our operations, making us a trusted name in the industry.

About 

Us

Our Mission

At CEOT Sumbak Farms, our mission is to provide both consumers and farmers with the highest quality goat breeds, Dorper sheep, and egg products. We are committed to excellence in every aspect of our operations, ensuring that our customers receive the best products at affordable prices. Through sustainable farming practices and a dedication to animal welfare, we aim to deliver exceptional value while promoting the health and well-being of our livestock and the satisfaction of our customers. We take pride in providing top-grade products for both local and international markets.

Our Vision

Our vision at CEOT Sumbak Farms is to become the leading provider of superior goat breeds, Dorper sheep, and egg products, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to set new standards in the farming industry, fostering a community where both consumers and farmers thrive. By continually innovating and adhering to the highest ethical practices, we aim to contribute to a healthier and more sustainable future for all. Our goal is to be a trusted name in the global market, delivering top-grade products that meet the needs of customers worldwide.
